Diving

Diving into the depth of the Inner Ocean
On the surface of events and impressions
Set in motion, it foams and bubbles and roars,
and if you stay on the surface of yourself,
you are tossed about by thoughts and emotions to and fro.
But if you dive into the the depth, you will find yourself.
